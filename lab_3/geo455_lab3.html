Lab 3: The Best National Parks in the United States

Coincidentally, many of the United State's greatest National Parks (N.P.) are home to the west. Thankfully, these wonders of the world have been preserved for us all to share the freedom of nature. In order of the greatest and most visited National Parks are:

  1. Yellowstone N.P.
  2. Yosemite N.P.
  3. Glacier N.P.
  4. Grand Canyon N.P.
  5. Zion N.P.
  6. Grand Teton N.P.
  7. Bryce Canyon N.P.
  8. Arches N.P.
  9. Rocky Mountain N.P.
  10. Sequoia N.P.
